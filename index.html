Who I am & What I do

With over 25 years of experience spanning politics, academia, business, and media, Phapano Pasha is a distinguished strategic communications specialist, political advisor, and thought leader. Her professional journey is defined by high-level strategic advisory in areas such as political campaigns, crisis management, confidential speechwriting, and stakeholder engagement for governments, institutions, and private clients. As the CEO of PM Pasha Consulting and Chairperson of the Centre for Alternative Political and Economic Thought, she leverages her expertise to drive innovative solutions and influence policy across Africa. At the core of her work is a steadfast belief in an Africa that leads with vision, resilience, and unity. Having transitioned from two decades of grassroots activism into business, Phapano's mission is to build a self-sufficient Africa that invests in its people, amplifies African voices, and reshapes global narratives about the continent. She is personally driven to use her platforms to nurture new leaders, influence policy, and ensure Africa’s voice matters in the shaping of tomorrow's world.
