Global Africa: African Voices Matter

Our Purpose, Vision & Mission

Purpose

Global Africa is a media company that was founded in 2023 by Phapano Phasha to deliberately drive a pro-Africa agenda.

Vision

To position Africa as a Global Superpower and to unashamedly posture the continent as a significant player by showcasing the achievements and contributions of Africans on a global scale.

Mission

To elevate voices that are often marginalised in mainstream media and showcase the diverse and vibrant stories of Africa.
