Global Africa: African Voices Matter

Our Purpose

Global Africa was founded to deliberately drive a pro-Africa agenda, challenging the prevalent narrative that often portrays the continent through a lens of poverty, corruption, and despair. Our mission is to establish a powerful online media house that tells and amplifies the remarkable achievements of African professionals, academics, and business leaders.

The platform is a digital media hub dedicated to elevating African voices and providing credible news. By promoting a *pan-Africanist yet global agenda*, we aim to reposition Africa as a key player in global affairs. The vision is to reshape how the world perceives Africa and how Africa perceives itself.

This advocacy is deeply linked to the *Global South and BRICS vision*. Ms. Pasha is also the Chair of The Centre for Alternative and Economic Thought, which steers cutting-edge research on the Global South and Africa's positioning in the global economy. The center focuses on a multipolar world, promoting economic partnerships and influencing policy that assists countries in the Global South to take advantage of BRICS member states. This initiative, therefore, serves to not only empower African voices but also to build the continent's strategic autonomy within a redefined global order.
