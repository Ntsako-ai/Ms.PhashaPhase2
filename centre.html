Our Mission & Goals

Mission

Driving catalytic ideas in the area of political and economic research in pursuit of alternative strategies and sharing of knowledge for development and prosperity in the global South.

Goals

  • Rethinking conventional ideas and creating new paradigms in the interest of political and economic prosperity for all.
  • Drive collaboration and knowledge creation between key relevant stakeholders in the Global South and BRICS countries.
  • Influence decision makers in particular heads of State to put their people and country first.
  • Develop policies which can assist countries in the Global South to harness their population as human capital.

Research Focus Areas

Our research is centered on uncovering untapped potentials and addressing existing gaps in the political and economic systems of the global South.

Comparative analysis of political structures and economic models in the global South and within the BRICS nations.

Identification of opportunities for sustainable development and growth in emerging markets.

Exploring innovative policy solutions for socio-economic challenges in the global South.

Examination of international relations dynamics and partnerships among BRICS countries and between countries in the global South.

How the West reports about the South and how the South reports about itself and the role of the mainstream media in shaping narratives about the world.
